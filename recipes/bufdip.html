Buffalo Dip

Description

Buffalo dip is a great party snack!

Great football game food

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken
  • 8 ounce package of cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup blue cheese salad dressing
  • 1/2 cup hot pepper sauce
  • 1/2 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/4 cup ranch dressing
Steps:
  1. Stir chicken, cream cheese, blue cheese dressing, hot pepper sauce, crumbled blue cheese, and ranch dressing together in a slow cooker.
  2. Cook in the slow cooker on high until heated through, about 20 minutes
